About Digital Mission

Academic Peer Learning Model

Designed by the Department of Computer Science at CHRIST, the Digital Mission provides technical skills empowerment. PG/PhD student mentors are trained to deliver workshops to non-computing scholars, building workspace capability and technological confidence.

L1

Level 1 - Essentials

Covers basic tools including Google Workspace collaboration, Canva visual design, and introductory computing logic to establish baseline digital confidence.

L2

Level 2 - Enterprise

Deep dive into professional scripting with Python, data modeling, AWS Cloud fundamentals, and applied generative AI to prepare scholars for placement.

✓

Springboard Integration

Aligned with digital content from partner initiatives like Infosys Springboard. All coursework culminates in verified student certificates.
